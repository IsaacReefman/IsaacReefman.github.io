Wedding Music

Music can bring warmth, beauty, and a sense of presence to a wedding — whether it’s a full liturgical celebration, a garden ceremony, or something uniquely your own. With experience across sacred and secular settings, I offer tailored musical services that reflect each couple’s story and the tone of their day. From timeless hymns and classical pieces to contemporary love songs and custom arrangements, I work closely with clients to shape a musical experience that’s elegant, heartfelt, and seamlessly integrated into the flow of the celebration. For those seeking a fuller sound, I can also coordinate additional musicians — subject to availability — to suit the scale and style of your event.
