Funeral Music

Music can offer comfort, dignity, and a sense of presence in moments of farewell. With years of experience performing for funerals across parishes, chapels, and private venues, I provide music that’s both heartfelt and appropriate — whether traditional hymns, contemporary sacred songs, or instrumental arrangements tailored to your preferences. I work closely with families and funeral directors to ensure the music supports the tone and flow of the service, and I’m happy to advise on selections that reflect the life and faith of your loved one.
